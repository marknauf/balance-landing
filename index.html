Finding Balance

Balance uses easy to use, and developmentally appropriate technology to meaningfully improve total well-being of youth by tracking and addressing those things that have the greatest impact on physical and mental health.

Fun and simple app features

Identify trends and triggers in your or your child’s day with easy tracking that can be done from your phone or tablet. Easy to read display and colorful emojis engage and empower youth to take control of their health and well-being.

How can this app help to improve health and well-being?

Balance uses mood + activity + sleep to determine total well-being. Personalized Balance scores can be reviewed daily, weekly, and monthly to identify patterns over time. Patterns help you and your child understand what things help and hurt overall wellness.

Will my child be able to use this app?

The Balance App uses pictures and emojis to engage and encourage users with in-app’s activities. We built a design to empower youth. Users input their own data and have fun while understanding their own health and wellness. The easy to navigate features use images familiar to kids and teens, swipe and click technology, a scoring system. Whether you’re a parent, a caretaker, or provider, this app can empower your child to understand their total health and well-being.
